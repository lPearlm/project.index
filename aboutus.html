Our Mission

At Mykonos Swimwear, we are reimagining swimwear as a catalyst for positive environmental change. Our mission is to create high-performance, elegantly designed swimwear that not only makes you look and feel incredible but also protects the very oceans and ecosystems that inspire our craft.

We are committed to the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals, particularly Goal 12 (Responsible Consumption and Production) and Goal 14 (Life Below Water). Each piece we create is a testament to our belief that fashion can be a powerful force for environmental stewardship. By sourcing regenerated marine plastics, utilizing innovative eco-friendly fabrics, and maintaining rigorous ethical manufacturing standards, we transform waste into beautiful, functional swimwear.

Quality is at the heart of everything we do. Our garments are meticulously designed to withstand the demands of active ocean lovers while maintaining exceptional comfort, durability, and style. We invest in advanced sustainable technologies and partner with responsible manufacturers who share our vision of creating clothing that respects both people and planet. Through transparent supply chains, circular design principles, and ongoing environmental initiatives, we aim to inspire a global community of conscious consumers who understand that every purchase is an opportunity to support marine conservation and sustainable fashion. Oceanic Threads isn't just a swimwear brand—we're a movement dedicated to protecting our planet's most precious resource: its oceans.
